Foraminis is a strategy game.

How it goes ?

You have a pawn and you need to run over the adverse pawns.

Each movement bring them towards you, be cautious.

How do you play ?

You have to press any directional key to move in the chosen direction.

You can also press simultaneously the shift key to double the motion.
